# OpenClaw Newsroom — Editorial Profile

## Focus Areas
- Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning breakthroughs
- Large Language Models (LLMs) — new releases, benchmarks, fine-tuning
- Open-source AI projects and tools
- AI infrastructure, MLOps, and deployment
- AI policy, regulation, and safety
- Developer tools and programming languages
- Cloud computing and distributed systems

## Excluded Topics
- Cryptocurrency price speculation and trading
- Consumer gadget reviews (phones, laptops) unless AI-related
- Celebrity tech gossip
- Generic corporate press releases with no substance
- Clickbait listicles ("Top 10 AI tools you NEED")

## Preferred Sources (higher trust)
- TechCrunch, Ars Technica, The Verge
- Hacker News (community-validated)
- arXiv papers (when accessible)
- Official company engineering blogs
- GitHub trending repositories

## Tone & Style
- Analytical and concise — no hype
- Explain why a story matters to practitioners
- Highlight technical details over marketing claims
- Group related stories into coherent narratives
- Be skeptical of benchmarks without reproducibility
